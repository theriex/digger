Digger Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will help you understand how epinova consulting ("us", "we", "our") uses and protects the data you provide to us when you visit and use DiggerHub.com ("website", "service") directly or through the Digger app.

What User Data We Collect

When you use the service, we may collect the following data:

  • Your IP address and other standard web request information.
  • Your first name (whatever name by which you wish to be called), a unique digger name, your service user ID, your email address, and a hash of your password.
  • Song or bookmark data you save including
    • Identifying information consisting of two or more values for title, artist, album, URL, file path, or service ID.
    • Supporting descriptive information consisting of music file metadata values and/or references to associated images.
    • Rating information you have set for energy level, approachability, keywords, star value, type, collection status, and personal notes.
    • Playback information indicating how often you have indicated a song should play, when it was last played, if it played through, how many times it has been played.
    • Recommendation source information may include text, or an id value referencing another user, source, or song,

Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for several reasons:

  • To ensure your data is only updated by you, and to communicate with you if there is a problem with your account.
  • To communicate with you if you request an email message from the service.
  • To facilitate selection of music to play, and make your music impressions available to you across different platforms.
  • To facilitate collaboration and sharing between you and others.

Safeguarding and Securing the Data

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Our Cookie Policy

When you sign in, your authentication information is saved in a local browser cookie to allow access to the site. When you sign out, this cookie is deleted. No cookie information is saved on the server or shared with other sites.

Your browser settings may allow you to automatically generate a "Do Not Track" signal. The service does not engage in tracking, so it behaves the same whether you send this signal or not.

Links To Other Websites

Our website contains links to other websites. Those websites are not governed by this privacy policy, and we are not responsible for your data and privacy protection on those sites. Make sure to read the privacy policy documentation of any website you go to from our website.

Restricting the Collection of Your Personal Data

We do not sell your private information. We do not share your private information except as required by law.

You have the right to access your personal data, and the right to rectify your data if it is inaccurate or processed unfairly. Refer to the website for contact and operational information.
