Digger Project Principles:

  • Just press play: Set your filters and enjoy your music. Support album playback, search, history, deck tuning and other features for active listening.
  • Trustable OPENSOURCE: See how Digger works and what it does.
  • Minimal data: Save only what is needed for song impressions, authentication, and structured collaboration. Leave chat to social media or personal communications.
  • Respect for music: Enhance music library access. Don't modify music files. Support and add value for artists and vendors.
  • Optional hub: Support running standalone, or with no network connection.
