Digger Support

If you have run into an issue with Digger on any platform, consider searching the ISSUESONGITHUB and adding a comment if found, or opening a new issue as needed. If you prefer, you can also contact SUPPEMAIL to get in touch. For sponsored development, donations, or other inquiries contact EPINOVA.

Project Principles:

  • Just press play: Play your music by mixing and tuning. Support search but don't require it.
  • Trustable OPENSOURCE: See how Digger works and what it does.
  • Minimal data: Save only what is needed for song impressions, collaboration, and authentication.
  • All about the music: Collaborate directly from personal music impressions. Leave chat to social connection venues.
  • Respect for music: Enhance music library access. Don't modify music files. Support and add value for artists and vendors.
  • Opt-in hub: Support running standalone, or with no network connection.
